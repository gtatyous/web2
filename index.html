Who We Are.

Design Team 1 is a professional design group full of bright, young minds. Composed of five members, Design Team 1 strives to revolutionize the field this life-changing project.

Adam Stowe.

Adam is from Grand Rapids, Michigan. He is graduating with a degree in Electrical Engineering in May 2015. He is pending approval for a position as a Nuclear Propulsion Engineer for the United States Navy.

Jose Carmona.

Jose, from Sturgis, Michigan is expected to graduate with a Bachelor's of Science in Electrical Engineering in December of 2015. Jose will be returning to Bosch for an internship and is looking forward to getting more experience in the field.

Kasey Durkin.

Kasey is from Carleton, Michigan and will be graduating with a Bachelor's of Science in Electrical Engineering in May 2015. She is very excited to have accepted a position with Eaton as a Power Systems Engineer at their New Jersey location.

Parker Mojsiejenko.

Parker is from Bridgman, Michigan. He is graduating with a degree in Electrical Engineering in May 2015. He is currently exploring career options and is extremely excited to apply his strengths in the future.

Stephanie Le.

Originally from Holt, Michigan, Stephanie plans on graduating with a Bachelor's of Science in Electrical Engineering in May 2015. She has accepted a position with Jackson National Life Insurance as a Software Developer I and looks forward to starting her new position upon completion of her degree.
